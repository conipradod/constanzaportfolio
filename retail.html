Retail

Redesigning price management — users at the core of automation

2019–2021

#ServiceDesign  ·  #TechDriven  ·  #DataScience  ·  #Automatization  ·  #ChangeManagement

The Challenge

Thousands of price changes. Hundreds of stores. Three teams that never "talked" to each other.

The complexity wasn't just technical — it was organizational, cultural, and deeply human.

A retailer flying blind.

One of Latin America's largest retailers — 500+ stores, thousands of weekly price changes — running its entire pricing operation through spreadsheets, WhatsApp, and gut feel. My job was to change that.

My role: UX Designer  ·  Service Design  ·  Product Design

500+ stores impacted
3 user types, distinct needs
2 products designed from scratch

Who I designed for

Planners

Simulate price scenarios and plan weekly changes across product categories.

Product Managers

Own product strategy and collections. Need full visibility across the pricing pipeline.

Store Assistants

Apply price changes on the floor in real time — thousands of products, same day.

The Foundation

I went to the stores. No assumptions.

While everyone was debating features, I was in the stores watching what actually happened when a price change arrived. The gap between the system and reality was larger than anyone admitted.

The Methodology

How I Mapped the Problem

Before designing a single screen, I mapped every step, every role, and every handoff in the price change chain. Eight activities. Five user types. Dozens of failure points. This map became the strategic foundation for every decision we made.

My Key Contribution

I mapped every role, dependency and failure point — then designed the orchestrator that made the whole system work.

The orchestrator is a semi-automated workflow tool that tracks the maturity level of each week's pricing process against predefined time limits — alerting on errors and keeping all stakeholders in sync.

Two products delivered*

01 Back-Office Dashboard

End-to-end planning tool for Planners and PMs to simulate price scenarios, forecast revenue impact, and manage weekly pricing decisions.

Price simulation  ·  Revenue forecasting  ·  Weekly planning  ·  Error tracking

02 Store App & Dashboard

Simple, fast tool for store staff to receive weekly price updates, log implementation, and upload photo proof — all in one place.

Real-time updates  ·  Photo proof  ·  Implementation log  ·  Store dashboard
